Dubai is a modern and luxurious city in the United Arab Emirates.
It is famous for its tall skyscrapers, beautiful beaches, and large shopping malls.
One of the most popular attractions is the Burj Khalifa, the tallest building in the world.
Dubai is also known for its desert adventures, rich culture, and international lifestyle.
Millions of tourists visit the city every year to enjoy its unique mix of tradition and modernity.
